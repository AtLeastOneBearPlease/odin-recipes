Chicken Stir Fry

Description

This recipe is bursting with flavor but so simple to make. No need to grab take out when you can make this amazing dish in just minutes.

This recipe is the result of me throwing stuff together one night for dinner. I was actually going to make something else and didnâ€™t have all the ingredients. Next thing I know, this Quick and Easy Chicken Stir Fry Recipe was born and it was so tasty! Even better, this skillet recipe is very frugal. The entire dish has a really good flavor with the blend of soy sauce and honey. The sweet and savory chicken is so flavorful when combined with these easy ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken breast
  • 1 cup dry rice
  • 1 (10oz) bag frozen stir fry veggies
  • 4 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 Tbsp minced ginger
  • 1 12oz container G Hughes sweet chili sauce
    • PF Chang sweet chili also work
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp siracha

Steps

  • Dice chicken into bite sized pieces and add to mixing bowl with sesame oil, garlic, giner, and listed seasonings (onion powder, black pepper, red pepper flakes, siracha)
  • To a pan on medium-high heat with cooking spray, add frozen vegetables and let cook for 8-10 minutes until softened then remove.
  • Add chicken to pan and cook on medium-high heat for 10-12 minutes or until chicken is completely cooked
  • Add 2 cups of water to a large pot on high heat with dry rice, bring to a boil, then cover, turn the heat to low, and let cook for an additional 10 minutes
  • Return veggies with chicken and add sweet chili sauce
  • Split rice and chicken into 6 meal prep containers
